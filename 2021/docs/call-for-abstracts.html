ERU SYMPOSIUM 2021 - CALL FOR ABSTRACTS

Annual ERU Research Symposium is organized as an local virtual event parallel to the ERU's flagship event - MERCon 2021. ERU Symposium provides an excellent opportunity for academics, industry practitioners, and students to present their research.

Submit Extended Abstracts

ERU 2021 accepts extended abstracts of research under following tracks:

  • Chemical and Process Engineering
  • Civil Engineering
  • Computer Science and Engineering
  • Earth Resources Engineering
  • Electrical Engineering
  • Electronic and Telecommunication Engineering
  • Materials Science and Engineering
  • Mechanical Engineering
  • Transport and Logistics Management
  • Textile and Clothing Technology

Please find the important dates of the Annual ERU Research Symposium as follows;

  • Extended Abstract Submission: 29th March 2021 03rd May 2021 15th June 2021 26th June 2021 (final)
  • Acceptance Notification: 14th June 2021 28th June 2021
  • Final Submission and Registration: 14th July 2021
  • Symposium: 30th July 2021 (Virtual)
