Registration

Registration

Author Registration and Payment Deadline: 14th July 2021

  • Register via https://bit.ly/MERCon2021-AR.
  • Arrange the publication payment and e-mail proof of payment (bank slip, online notice, EDAS for foreign affiliations, etc) to mercon@uom.lk including paper ID and Title to complete the registration by 14th July 2021.

Participant Registration (free)

  • Register via https://bit.ly/MERCon2021-PR.

Instructions for registrations and payment details are given below,

  • Reduced registration fee will be charged as a publication fee for the Virtual form of the conference.
  • At least one author should register and pay the publication fee.
  • All other participants including authors are given free registrations.
  • All registrations include electronic form of conference proceedings, access to conference portal, access to keynote and technical sessions in online and access to workshops in online.
  • Please note that ONE author registration (i.e. Publication fee) will cover only ONE paper.

  • Publication fees are given below.
Author Category Fee
Authors with local affiliations and IEEE membership LKR 13,000/- per paper
Authors from University of Moratuwa, Sri Lanka LKR 13,000/- per paper
Other authors with other Local affiliations LKR 15,000/- per paper
Authors with international affiliations and IEEE membership USD 100 per paper
Other authors with international affiliations USD 125 per paper
Registration fees for all other participants (students, academia, industry, and other authors) Free


Payment Method

You can pay via one of the following options:

  • For Authors with Sri Lankan Affiliations
    • Option 1: You may pay to any Bank of Ceylon branch or pay via online banking facilities. Use following details:
      • A/C No – 306836
      • A/C Name – University of Moratuwa
      • Branch – Bank of Ceylon, Katubedda
      • Clearly indicate your Name and Telephone number. Use “MERCon 2021-EDAS paper ID” as reason/memo for payment. Please ask the bank teller to print these on deposit slip. If you are transferring via online banking, indicate the reason as “MERCon 2021-EDAS paper ID”.
    • Option 2: To Shroff counter of the University of Moratuwa. Counter is open on weekdays from 9.00 am - 12.30 pm and 1.30 pm - 3.00 pm. (may not be opened as usual or you may not allowed to enter the university premises based on the situation of the country.)

  • For Authors with Foreign Affiliations
    • Can pay via EDAS. Click on “ register for conference” in papers page of EDAS.
  • Note:
    • Please contact registration chair for any clarification. Email: rasaras@uom.lk, mercon@uom.lk
    • Direct reimbursement from Staff Development Center (SDC) of University of Moratuwa is not available. Staff members of University of Moratuwa can pay the registration fees and then reimburse the same from SDC. See details at https://uom.lk/sdc/application-form.
