Call for Papers

MERCon 2024 is the 10th International Conference organized by the Engineering Research Unit, University of Moratuwa, Sri Lanka. We welcome research papers on a broad range of research themes, including both theoretical and applied research. The following engineering-related disciplines are the focus of the research papers that will be presented at MERCon 2024.

Submission Deadline: April 5, 2024

Extended Deadline: April 19, 2024 April 26, 2024

Please Follow The Paper Template At: https://www.ieee.org/conferences/publishing/templates.html
